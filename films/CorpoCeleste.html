99 minutes

Director Alice Rohrwacher
Year 2011
Country France, Italy, Switzerland
Genre Drama

After growing up in Switzerland, 13-year-old Marta returns to a city in southern Italy with her mother and older sister. Independent and inquisitive, she joins a catechism class at a local church. However, the games and religious pop songs she encounters there do not nearly satisfy her interest in faith.
