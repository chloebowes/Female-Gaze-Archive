The Bride

73 minutes

Director Myriam Uwiragiye Birara
Year 2023
Country Rwanda
Genre Drama

Three years after the Genocide Against Tutsis, Eva, a young woman with dreams of going to medical school is abducted and subjected to, Guterura, a form of traditional marriage. Feeling abandoned by her conservative family who pushes her to accept her new situation, Eva bonds with the cousin of her abductor.
