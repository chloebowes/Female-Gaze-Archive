Sugar Cane Alley

103 minutes

Director Euzhan Palcy
Year 1983
Country France, Martinique
Genre Drama

Martinique, in the early 1930s. Young José and his grandmother live in a small village. Nearly everyone works cutting cane and barely earning a living. The overseer can fine a worker for the smallest infraction. The way to advance is to do well in school. José studies hard and succeeds in an exam allowing him to attend school in the capital.
