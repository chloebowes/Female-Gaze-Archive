Songs My Brother Taught Me

98 minutes

Director Chloe Zhao
Year 2015
Country USA
Genre Drama

Leyla, a lawyer and a poet, takes the long-distance train to attend her high school reunion dinner. On the train, she meets Canan, a young nursing student in distress. As the conversation develops, Leyla learns that Canan is travelling to assist with the suicide of a paralysed man, Yavuz, who wants to die.
