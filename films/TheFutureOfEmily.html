The Future Of Emily

106 minutes

Director Helma Sanders-Brahams
Year 1984
Country France, Germany
Genre Drama

Emily, a child, stays with her bourgeois grandparents during frequent periods when her mother makes films. Isabelle wraps a picture, flies to her childhood home to pick up Emily, and plans to leave for her place in France. Old wounds between Isabelle and her parents open around Isabelle’s life style. It’s also apparent that Isabelle’s mother, Paula, is unhappy - with her husband and with her youthful hopes dashed when she became pregnant with Isabelle. …more
