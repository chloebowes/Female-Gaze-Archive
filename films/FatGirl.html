Fat Girl

86 minutes

Director Catherine Breillat
Year 2001
Country France, Italy, Switzerland
Genre Drama

Anaïs is twelve and bears the weight of the world on her shoulders. She watches her older sister, Elena, whom she both loves and hates. Elena is fifteen and devilishly beautiful, she cannot understand that she is merely an object of desire. And, as such, she can only be taken. Indeed, this is the subject: a girl’s loss of virginity. And, that summer, it opens a door to tragedy.
