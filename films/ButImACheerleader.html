But I'm a Cheerleader

85 minutes

Director Jamie Babbit
Year 1999
Country USA
Genre Comedy, Romance, Coming of Age

Megan is an all-American girl. A cheerleader. She has a boyfriend. But Megan doesn’t like kissing her boyfriend very much. Her conservative parents worry that she must be a lesbian and send her off to “sexual redirection” school, where she must learn how to be straight.
