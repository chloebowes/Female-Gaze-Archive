Kama Sutra: A Tale of Love

117 minutes

Director Mira Nair
Year 1996
Country India, UK, USA
Genre Drama, Romance

Tara and Maya are two inseparable friends in India. The two best friends turn into enemies when Tara becomes engaged to King Raj Singh. Embittered by Tara's sudden poor treatment of her, Maya entices Raj, who is too distracted by hedonistic pursuits to realize his kingdom is in danger.
